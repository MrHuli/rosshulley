UK Guesser!

Guess where you are in the UK using Google street view!

Places you in a random street view location in the UK, and from here you have to guess which town you are in

Originally picking a random Latitude and Longitude in the UK and putting you on the nearest street view, this almost always ended in the player being on a nondescript country lane! Updated now to only include major towns and cities in the UK, plus some personal locations :).

A learning exercise and an example of using Google maps API, inspired by geoguessr.

Sorting Algorithms

A visualisation of sorting algorithms, now with sound! (WARNING, LOUD) - NOTE, due to a bug in chrome sound has been disabled :(

Each one of these creates a random array of numbers, uses these to determine the hight of several bars, these bars are then sorted from shortest to highest.

Every time a piece is moved, a sound wave is generated, with the height of the bar determining the frequency of the wave.

  • Bubble Sort
  • Radix Sort
  • Insertion Sort
  • Cocktail Shaker Sort

Bouncy balls

Collisions and animations

Multicoloured bouncy balls. Click and drag to add more balls to the screen. Working with gravity and momentum, balls lose a little on each bounce

Demonstration of a flexible canvas (resize it!) and touch events etc, and works with the accelerometer in iPads, turn it and the balls always fall down.

Shake the iPad to remove balls.
